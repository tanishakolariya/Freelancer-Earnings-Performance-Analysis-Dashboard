# Freelancer Earnings & Performance Analysis Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing freelancer earnings, job performance, and platform trends across global markets.

##  Overview
This dashboard explores freelancer income and performance data across multiple platforms, job categories, client regions, and payment methods — helping identify which platforms, job types, and regions offer the best earning potential and success rates.

##  Tools Used
- **Power BI** — dashboard design and visualization
- **DAX** — calculated measures (average earnings, success rate, rehire rate)
- **Power Query** — data cleaning and transformation

## Data Source
Dataset: Freelancer Earnings and Job Trends (Kaggle)

##  Key Insights
- Total freelancer income analyzed: **$9.78M+**
- Average hourly rate: **$52.58**
- Average job success rate: **74.95%**
- **Upwork** and **Toptal** show the highest success rates among all platforms
- Earnings are fairly distributed across platforms, with Freelancer.com and PeoplePerHour showing slightly lower shares
- Client regions (USA, Europe, Asia, Canada, UK, Australia) show consistent hourly rate patterns across payment methods


##  Dashboard Features
- KPI cards: Average Hourly Rate, Total Income, Job Success Rate
- Slicers: Client Rating, Experience Level (Beginner/Intermediate/Expert)
- Freelancer job category & earnings breakdown
- Earnings by platform (pie chart) and by country (bar chart)
- Hourly rate by client region, segmented by payment method
- Rehire rate vs. job success rate trend by job type
- Success rate by platform (trend line)
